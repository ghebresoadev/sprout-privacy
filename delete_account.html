Delete Account & Data Request

You have the right to request deletion of your SPROUT account and all associated personal data. This page explains how to submit a deletion request and what data will be removed.

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your progress, grades, attendance records, and certificates will be permanently deleted.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1 — From Within the App (Recommended)

Step 1: Open SPROUT and sign in to your account
Step 2: Go to your Dashboard → Settings
Step 3: Tap Profile → Delete Account
Step 4: Confirm deletion — your account will be deleted within 30 days

Option 2 — Email Request

Send an email to: ghebresoadev@gmail.com

Subject: SPROUT Account Deletion Request

Include:
  • Your full name
  • Your registered email address
  • Your church name and church code
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation email.

What Data is Deleted

🗑️ Permanently Deleted:
  • Your name and email address
  • Your password and login credentials
  • Your profile photo
  • Your quiz scores and answers
  • Your attendance records
  • Your lesson notes and bookmarks
  • Your flashcards and study plans
  • Your prayer wall posts
  • Your messages with instructors
  • Your feedback reports
  • Your device push notification token
📋 Data That May Be Retained:
  • Anonymised statistical data used for church reporting (no personal identifiers)
  • Certificates already issued — copies may be retained by your church administrator
  • Financial records required by law (if any transactions were made — SPROUT has no purchases)

Retained data contains no personal identifiers and cannot be linked back to you.

Data Deletion Timeline

ActionTimeline

Personal data deleted from FirestoreWithin 30 days
Firebase Auth account deletedWithin 30 days
Confirmation email sentWithin 30 days
Backup data purgedWithin 90 days

Delete Only Your Data (Without Deleting Account)

If you want to delete specific data without closing your account (for example, delete your prayer wall posts or profile photo), you can:

  • Profile photo: Go to Profile → tap photo → Remove photo
  • Prayer wall posts: Go to Prayer Wall → tap your post → Delete
  • Lesson notes: Go to Notebook → swipe to delete individual notes
  • Flashcards: Go to Flashcards → My Cards → tap delete icon

Or email us at ghebresoadev@gmail.com with the specific data you want deleted.
