Summary: SPROUT is a Sunday School management app for the Eritrean Orthodox Tewahdo Church. We collect only the information needed to operate the app. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. Children's data is protected with extra care.

1. Who We Are

SPROUT is operated by Holy Trinity Eritrean Orthodox Tewahdo Church, Chicago, Illinois, USA. SPROUT is a non-commercial, faith-based educational platform serving Sunday School students, instructors, parents, and administrators of the Eritrean Orthodox Tewahdo Church (EOTC).

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your location or GPS data
  • We do not collect payment or financial information
  • We do not collect contacts from your phone
  • We do not collect browsing history
  • We do not use advertising tracking or analytics SDKs
  • We do not use cookies

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your SPROUT account
  • To display your lessons, grades, attendance, and progress
  • To allow instructors to manage their Sunday School class
  • To allow parents to monitor their linked child's progress
  • To send push notifications about announcements and lessons
  • To generate certificates of achievement
  • To allow administrators to manage churches within their diocese

5. How We Store Your Data

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore), a cloud database service operated by Google LLC. Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS and encrypted at rest by Firebase.

Your data is stored on Google's servers in the United States. By using SPROUT you agree to this storage location.

6. Who Can See Your Data

Your DataWho Can See It
Student grades and attendanceThe student themselves, their instructor, their linked parent
Profile informationYou, your church instructor and administrator
Prayer wall postsAll members of your church (can be posted anonymously)
Church dataOnly members of the same church
Diocese dataOnly Diocese and HQ administrators

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for commercial purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

SPROUT serves Sunday School students including children under 13. We take children's privacy very seriously.

  • Children's accounts are created by their parents or instructors
  • A child's data is only visible to their instructor and linked parent
  • We do not show advertising to any user, especially children
  • We do not collect more information from children than is necessary
  • Parents may request deletion of their child's account at any time by contacting us
  • Children cannot make in-app purchases — SPROUT has none

SPROUT complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable child privacy laws.

8. Data Retention

  • Your account data is retained while your account is active
  • Attendance and grade records are retained for the duration of your enrollment
  • If you request account deletion, your data is removed within 30 days
  • Anonymised statistical data may be retained for reporting purposes

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correct — update incorrect information through your profile
  • Delete — request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent — uninstall the app and request data deletion

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: ghebretesfamariam@gmail.com

10. Push Notifications

SPROUT sends push notifications for announcements, lesson updates, and attendance alerts. You can disable notifications at any time in your Android device Settings → Apps → SPROUT → Notifications.

11. Camera and Storage Permissions

  • Camera / Gallery: Used only when you choose to upload a profile photo or scan a QR code for attendance. We do not access your camera or photos without your action.
  • Storage: Used only to save generated PDF certificates to your device when you request them.
